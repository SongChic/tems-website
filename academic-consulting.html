Overview

TEMS Academy is focused on the future of our students. We offer services to set students on their career objectives early so they stay focused and achieve their post-secondary goals. Students in the Academic Consulting program will receive assistance with:

  • Goal setting in grades 9 and 10
  • Extracurricular activities management in grades 9 and 10
  • Volunteer hour selection
  • University research and program selection in grades 10 and 11
  • University application and form completion in grade 12
  • university essays and information forms in grade 12
