Introduction

New to Ontario? Find out more about the exciting Waterloo Region where TEMS is located. With innovative industries, a strong educational sector, and a growing economy, the Waterloo Region offers students a dynamic learning environment just a hour outside of Toronto. With over 50 schools, TEMS partners with the Waterloo Catholic District School Board to find students the perfect long-term study program. For Winter Schooling, TEMS works closely with WCDSB to ensure students are placed in classes with exemplary English learning environments to make the most of the short-term experience.
