Introduction

We offer lessons in all the core subjects, as well as individualized programs that target the specific needs of a student. Our group lessons are divided by grade range and/or ability level, so students are never placed in a lesson that is not relevant to their current learning in school. For students seeking enrichment opportunities we go beyond the traditional curriculum to provide more advanced work and detailed learning experiences.

All lessons come with homework support for the specific subject, and we also offer a quiet study hall where students may go to work on school projects and individual homework assignments.

Services

  • Remedial Tutoring
  • Enrichment Learning
  • Core Subjects and Specific Targets
  • Summer Programs

What Makes Us Different?

At TEMS Academy, our students are put first. Tutors collaborate in ideas and strategies in order to provide students with the best learning environment possible. We are able to be flexible with our scheduling and programs in order to best suit the needs of the student and his or her parents.

Introduction

Adult learning at TEMS is specifically tailored to the individual's needs. During the free consultation, adult students can discuss their goals, strengths and weaknesses and together with the coordinator they can decide if group classes are right for them or not. Adult classes are run for a variety of different reasons. Below are some of the more popular themed classes our adult students’ request:

  • Conversational ESL English
  • English Writing Improvement
  • Business English
  • IELTS/TOEFL Refresher Classes
  • Presentation skills for business
