Overview

During January and February, Canada TEMS Academy hosts international students in a winter immersion program that focuses on improving the students' English language skills. Students will attend a local school for the one to two months they are here, have additional ESL tutoring after school, and go on Saturday trips to local attractions. Students stay in our TEMS residence or with homestay families.

TEMS Winter Short-Term Schooling

  • Eligibility: Gr.5 – Gr.8
  • Program Length
    • Jan. 7, 2017 – Feb. 4, 2017 (4 weeks)

      • (Schedule could be changed because of flight ticket condition)

  • Day Schools: WCDSB Schools
  • After School Program: TEMS Academy
  • Accommodation: Canadian Homestay
  • Program Cost: $4500 CAD
    • Included in cost : Tuition fee, Homestay, Trip & Activity fee, Airport Service, Study materials, Custodianship Document Fee in Canada etc.
    • Not included in cost: Flight tickets, Travel Insurance
